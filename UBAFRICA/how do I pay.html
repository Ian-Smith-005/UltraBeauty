How to Pay with M-Pesa Till Number

  1. Step 1: Dial USSD Code
    1. On your mobile phone, open the dialer.
    2. Dial *334# and press the call button.
  2. Step 2: Select "Buy Goods and Services"
    1. From the menu options, choose "Buy Goods and Services" by typing the respective number and pressing "Send".

Important Tips:

  • Ensure you have sufficient funds in your M-Pesa account.
  • Double-check the Till Number and payment amount before confirming the transaction.
  • Always keep your M-Pesa PIN confidential.

For any issues or inquiries related to your payment, please contact the seller.
